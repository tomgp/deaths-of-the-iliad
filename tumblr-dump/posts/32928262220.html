As he raised that cry he flung his spear at Ajax— but the glinting metal missed and he hit Mastor’s son, Lycophron, Ajax’ friend in arms, Cythera-born yet he lived with Ajax once he’d killed a man on Cythera’s holy shores. Hector killed him now with whetted bronze, cleaving his skull above the ear as he stood by Ajax. Down off the ship’s stern he dropped, his back slamming the ground, his limbs slack in death
